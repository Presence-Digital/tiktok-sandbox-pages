Data Deletion Instructions

To delete all data associated with your account on the Presence platform:

  1. Go to the Brand Management section of the Presence Dashboard.
  2. Locate the Brand you wish to delete.
  3. Click the "Delete Brand" button.
  4. Check the box: "I want to also delete all engagements data stored in Presence."
  5. Confirm the deletion.

If you need manual deletion, please contact: support@yourdomain.com
